# Welcome to Kiwix SDL!

Your offline Wikipedia and ZIM archive reader is ready.

## How to use:
1. Download ZIM files (e.g. Wikipedia, Wikibooks, Wikivoyage, xkcd) from **kiwix.org**.
2. Copy the `.zim` files into the port directory:
   `/roms/ports/kiwix-sdl/`
3. Restart **Kiwix SDL**. It will automatically detect and load the archive!

## Controls:
* **D-Pad / Joystick**: Scroll and navigate links.
* **Button A / Enter**: Open selected link.
* **Button B / Backspace / Esc**: Go back in history.
* **Select / T**: Toggle the article search/index tree view.
* **Start / H**: Return to the main page.
* **Button D**: Toggle between Light and Dark theme.
